Atmosphere & Sound

Expect laid-back rhythms, imperfect recordings, and ambient layers that feel cozy and personal. Lo-fi is more about the *feeling* than the form.

Popular Uses

This genre has exploded in popularity through YouTube, Spotify, and Twitch as background music for studying, coding, reading, or simply zoning out.

Subtle Influence

Lo-fi blends elements from jazz, hip-hop, ambient, and even anime culture. It lives in the quiet moments and background vibes of digital life.
