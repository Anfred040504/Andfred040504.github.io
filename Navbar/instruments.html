Traditional Instruments

From violins and flutes to drums and pianos, traditional instruments are the foundation of classical music and many folk traditions. These instruments often require years of mastery, offering a unique, acoustic sound that remains timeless.

Modern Instruments

Today, technology has opened up new possibilities. Electric guitars, synthesizers, and digital keyboards have revolutionized music production. Modern instruments often blend traditional techniques with electronic components, creating endless sonic possibilities.
