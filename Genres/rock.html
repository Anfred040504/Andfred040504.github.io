Origins

Emerging in the 1950s from blues, jazz, and country roots, rock evolved with electric guitars, pounding drums, and raw vocals. It quickly became the voice of change and counterculture.

Signature Sound

Expect distortion, gritty riffs, energetic solos, and anthemic choruses. Subgenres like punk, metal, indie, and alt-rock expand the landscape, each with a unique attitude and vibe.

Influence

Rock has shaped fashion, politics, film, and generations of music lovers. From The Beatles to Nirvana, its rebellious DNA still lives in today's artists and fans.
